Chronology of Political Apologies

A comprehensive timeline of the official apologies issued up to date. The project realized in consultancy with Elazar Barkan and Graham G. Dodds and designed by Mahir M. Yavuz.

Darker color bars represent atrocities and their timeframe; colored and short bars represent the datestamp of the official apologies. Timeline does not contain information before 1900. Country names are grouped based on actual names of the countries today. Colors represent the degree of the apologies and (if exist) their consequences.
